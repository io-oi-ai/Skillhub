# Competitive Analysis Report

## Skill Overview

Understanding competitors is fundamental to formulating product and market strategies. This skill provides a systematic competitive research framework that helps product managers and marketers conduct comprehensive competitive analysis from multiple dimensions including product features, user experience, market positioning, and business models, producing well-structured analysis reports.

## Input Requirements

Please provide the following information:

- **Purpose of Analysis**: Why conduct this competitive analysis and what questions you want to answer
- **Your Product**: Brief introduction and current positioning of your product
- **Competitor List**: Competing products to analyze (recommended 2-5)
- **Key Focus Dimensions**: Such as feature comparison, pricing strategy, user experience, etc.
- **Existing Materials**: Any competitor information, industry reports already collected

## Analysis Framework

The competitive analysis will cover the following dimensions:

1. **Product Overview**: Competitor positioning, target users, core value proposition
2. **Feature Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison matrix of core features, highlighting differentiators
3. **User Experience**: Registration flow, core user journey, interaction design strengths and weaknesses
4. **Business Model**: Pricing model, pricing strategy, revenue streams
5. **Market Performance**: User scale, growth trends, market share (if publicly available)
6. **Technical Characteristics**: Technology architecture choices, performance metrics, open capabilities
7. **Operations Strategy**: Customer acquisition channels, content operations, community management approach
8. **Reputation Analysis**: High-frequency positive and negative review keywords from user feedback

## Output Content

- **Competitor Overview Table**: Summary of basic competitor information at a glance
- **Feature Comparison Matrix**: Feature availability comparison table organized by module
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats for each competitor
- **Differentiation Insights**: Market gaps and differentiation opportunity analysis
- **Strategic Recommendations**: Product and operations strategy recommendations based on analysis conclusions

## Usage Tips

- **Clarify your analysis purpose**: Whether finding differentiation opportunities or setting pricing benchmarks, different purposes require different focus
- **Update regularly**: Competitors continuously iterate; recommend updating analysis quarterly
- **Pay attention to user reviews**: App store comments and social media feedback often reveal more truth than official descriptions
- **Look beyond features**: Business models, operations strategies, team background and other "below the waterline" information are equally important
- **Avoid subjective bias**: Record facts objectively, clearly distinguish between analysis and viewpoints

## Applicable Scenarios

- Market research before launching a new product
- Competitive landscape analysis for annual product planning
- Competition positioning for investor pitching
- Market positioning reference before developing marketing strategies

# 中文版

# 竞争分析报告

## 技能概览

理解竞争对手是制定产品与市场策略的基础。本技能提供系统化的竞品研究框架，帮助产品经理和市场人员从产品功能、用户体验、市场定位与商业模式等多个维度开展全面竞品分析，并产出结构化的分析报告。

## 输入要求

请提供以下信息：

- **分析目的**：为何要做竞品分析，你希望回答哪些问题
- **你的产品**：产品简介与当前定位
- **竞品清单**：拟分析的竞品（建议 2-5 个）
- **关键关注维度**：如功能对比、定价策略、体验等
- **已有资料**：已收集的竞品信息、行业报告等

## 分析框架

竞品分析将覆盖以下维度：

1. **产品概览**：竞品定位、目标用户、核心价值
2. **功能对比**：核心功能对比矩阵，突出差异化
3. **用户体验**：注册流程、核心路径、交互优缺点
4. **商业模式**：定价模式、定价策略、收入来源
5. **市场表现**：用户规模、增长趋势、市场份额（如公开可得）
6. **技术特征**：技术架构选择、性能指标、开放能力
7. **运营策略**：获客渠道、内容运营、社区管理方式
8. **口碑分析**：用户反馈中的高频正/负面关键词

## 输出内容

- **竞品概览表**：竞品基本信息一览
- **功能对比矩阵**：按模块组织的功能对比表
- **SWOT 分析**：每个竞品的优势/劣势/机会/威胁
- **差异化洞察**：市场空白与差异化机会分析
- **策略建议**：基于结论的产品与运营建议

## 使用建议

- **先明确分析目的**：不同目的决定不同重点（差异化、定价对标等）
- **定期更新**：竞品迭代快，建议按季度更新
- **重视用户评论**：应用商店与社媒评论常比官方宣传更真实
- **不只看功能**：商业模式、运营策略、团队背景等“水下信息”同样关键
- **避免主观偏差**：事实与观点分离，保持客观

## 适用场景

- 新产品上线前的市场研究
- 年度产品规划的竞品分析
- 投资路演的竞争定位
- 制定市场策略前的定位参考
