# Product Hunt Launch Preparation

## Skill Overview

Product Hunt is one of the most important platforms for indie developers launching new products. A successful Product Hunt launch not only brings significant initial users but also attracts media attention and investor interest. However, many developers lack experience preparing launch materials, leading to suboptimal results. This skill helps you systematically prepare all copy materials needed for a Product Hunt launch and provides proven launch strategy.

## Input Requirements

Please provide the following information:

- **Product Name and Website**: What is your product called, and what is the official website URL
- **One-Liner Introduction**: Explain what your product does in the simplest language
- **Core Selling Points**: The product's most compelling 2-3 features
- **Target Audience**: Which members of the Product Hunt community would be interested in your product
- **Product Story** (optional): Why did you build this product, and any interesting development experiences
- **Available Data** (optional): User count, revenue, media coverage, or other achievements to showcase
- **Pricing Information**: Do you offer exclusive discounts for Product Hunt users

## Output Content

### 1. Tagline

- English tagline not exceeding 60 characters
- Concise and powerful, communicate product value in one sentence
- Provide 3-5 alternative options
- Avoid superlatives like "The best...", "World's first...", etc.

### 2. Description

- Short description within 260 characters
- Structure: Value proposition + Core features + Target users
- Written in natural, fluent English

### 3. Maker Comment

- The first comment posted after launch, from the maker
- Include the following elements:
  - Friendly greeting and self-introduction
  - Why you built this product (backstory)
  - Core product highlights
  - Exclusive benefits for the Product Hunt community
  - Invite feedback and questions
- Sincere and personal tone, avoid marketing speak

### 4. First Comment

- Suggestions for 1-2 follow-up comments
- Can supplement technical details, future plans, etc.
- Open-ended questions to guide community discussion

### 5. Gallery Description

- Content recommendations for 5 showcase images
- Each image's title and description
- Suggested display order (most important first)
- Image design tips

### 6. Topics

- Recommend 3-5 most relevant Product Hunt Topics
- Tag selection strategy explanation

### 7. Exclusive Offer Plan

- Discount recommendations for the Product Hunt community
- Discount code naming suggestions (e.g., PRODUCTHUNT2026)
- Time-limited strategy suggestions

## Launch Day Strategy

### Timing

- **Best Launch Time**: 00:01 Pacific Time (16:01 Beijing Time)
- **Best Launch Day**: Tuesday through Thursday; avoid weekends and holidays
- **Avoid**: Major tech announcement days like Apple keynote events

### Launch Day Timeline

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 hour before | Final review of all materials; confirm all links work |
| 0-1 hour after launch | Post Maker Comment; notify core supporters |
| 1-4 hours after | Actively reply to every comment; maintain engagement |
| 4-8 hours after | Share progress on social media; update metrics |
| 8-16 hours after | Continue engagement; post follow-up updates |
| 16-24 hours after | Thank the community; summarize achievements; plan next steps |

### Community Engagement Tips

- **Reply to Every Comment**: Each comment deserves a thoughtful response; this is core Product Hunt etiquette
- **Express Genuine Gratitude**: Thank everyone for upvotes and comments
- **No Vote Manipulation**: Product Hunt algorithms detect abnormal voting behavior and may lower your product's ranking
- **Share Behind-the-Scenes Stories**: The community loves seeing real product stories and data
- **Accept Criticism**: Stay open and humble to negative feedback; show willingness to improve

### Social Media Support

- Twitter/X post template
- LinkedIn post template
- Indie developer community (e.g., IndieHackers) sharing template

## Usage Tips

- **Pre-Warm Your Audience**: 1-2 weeks before launch, tease the product on social media to build anticipation
- **Prepare Your Ship Page**: Create a Ship page on Product Hunt early to collect subscribers
- **Find a Hunter**: Invite an influential Hunter to hunt your product rather than launching yourself
- **Gallery Quality is Critical**: Your first image determines whether users click; invest time in design
- **Prepare FAQ**: Anticipate community questions in advance and prepare answers
- **Stay Online All Day**: You need to be online throughout Launch Day for active engagement

# 中文版

# Product Hunt 上线准备

## 技能概览

Product Hunt 是独立开发者发布新品的重要平台。一场成功的上线不仅能带来初始用户，还可能吸引媒体与投资者关注。但很多开发者缺乏准备经验导致效果不佳。本技能帮助系统化准备上线所需文案，并提供验证过的上线策略。

## 输入要求

请提供以下信息：

- **产品名称与官网**：产品名称及官网链接
- **一句话简介**：用最简洁语言说明产品价值
- **核心卖点**：最具吸引力的 2-3 个功能
- **目标受众**：哪些 Product Hunt 用户会感兴趣
- **产品故事**（可选）：为什么做这个产品，开发经历
- **可展示数据**（可选）：用户数、收入、媒体报道等
- **定价信息**：是否提供专属折扣

## 输出内容

### 1. Tagline

- 英文 Tagline 不超过 60 字符
- 简洁有力，一句话传递价值
- 提供 3-5 个备选
- 避免使用“最好的/全球首个”等夸张词

### 2. Description

- 260 字符以内短描述
- 结构：价值主张 + 核心功能 + 目标用户
- 用自然流畅的英文表述

### 3. Maker Comment

- 上线后第一条官方评论
- 包含：
  - 友好问候与自我介绍
  - 产品起因与故事
  - 核心亮点
  - 面向社区的专属福利
  - 邀请反馈与提问
- 语气真诚，避免营销腔

### 4. First Comment

- 建议 1-2 条后续评论
- 可补充技术细节或未来计划
- 使用开放性问题引导讨论

### 5. Gallery 说明

- 5 张展示图的内容建议
- 每张图的标题与描述
- 推荐展示顺序（最重要的在前）
- 设计建议

### 6. Topics

- 推荐 3-5 个最相关 Topics
- 标签选择策略说明

### 7. 专属优惠方案

- 面向 Product Hunt 社区的折扣建议
- 折扣码命名建议（如 PRODUCTHUNT2026）
- 限时策略建议

## 上线日策略

### 时间选择

- **最佳上线时间**：太平洋时间 00:01（北京时间 16:01）
- **最佳上线日**：周二至周四，避开周末与节假日
- **避免**：大型科技发布日（如苹果发布会）

### 上线日时间线

| 时间 | 行动 |
|------|------|
| 上线前 1 小时 | 最终检查材料，确认链接可用 |
| 上线后 0-1 小时 | 发布 Maker Comment，通知核心支持者 |
| 上线后 1-4 小时 | 积极回复评论，保持互动 |
| 上线后 4-8 小时 | 社媒分享进展，更新数据 |
| 上线后 8-16 小时 | 持续互动，发布跟进更新 |
| 上线后 16-24 小时 | 感谢社区，总结成果，规划下一步 |

### 社区互动建议

- **回复每条评论**：这是核心礼仪
- **真诚感谢**：感谢每一次点赞与反馈
- **禁止刷票**：平台会识别异常投票并降权
- **分享幕后故事**：社区喜欢真实数据与经历
- **接受批评**：保持开放谦逊，展现改进意愿

### 社媒支持

- Twitter/X 模板
- LinkedIn 模板
- IndieHackers 等社区分享模板

## 使用建议

- **提前预热**：上线前 1-2 周在社媒预告
- **准备 Ship Page**：尽早创建并收集订阅
- **找 Hunter**：邀请影响力 Hunter 代发
- **图库质量很关键**：首图决定点击率
- **准备 FAQ**：提前准备社区问题的回答
- **当天保持在线**：上线日需持续互动
