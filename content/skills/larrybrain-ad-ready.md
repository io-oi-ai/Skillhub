# Ad Ready - AI Ad Image Generator

## Overview

Ad Ready is an automated ad image generation tool from LarryBrain. Simply provide a product page link, and the system automatically extracts product information and visual assets, then generates professional advertising images that comply with the specifications of major advertising platforms.

Say goodbye to expensive designer outsourcing and complex Photoshop operations. Get production-ready ad assets in just minutes.

## Core Features

### Intelligent Asset Extraction

- Input a product URL and automatically extract product title, description, price, key selling points, and images
- Supports major ecommerce platforms: Amazon, Shopify, Taobao, independent stores, and more
- Automatically removes watermarks and backgrounds to extract clean product images
- Identifies core product benefits for ad copywriting

### Multi-Platform Ad Formats

- **Facebook / Instagram Ads**: Standard sizes including 1080x1080, 1080x1920, 1200x628, and more
- **Google Display Ads**: Covers all common display network sizes
- **TikTok Ads**: Vertical full-screen and feed ad formats
- **Pinterest Promoted Pins**: Long-form and standard ratio formats
- Generate all platform-required sizes in one go, no manual adjustments needed

### Professional Design Templates

- Built-in dozens of proven high-converting ad design templates
- Covers multiple styles: minimalist, promotional, comparison, story-driven, testimonials
- Automatic color schemes extracted from product images to match brand colors
- Typography automatically optimized for mobile screen readability

### Batch Generation & A/B Testing

- Automatically generate multiple design variations for the same product
- Support batch importing product links to process hundreds of SKUs at once
- Provide different copywriting and visual approaches for each ad set for testing
- Export formats support PNG and JPG with resolutions meeting platform requirements

## Workflow

1. **Input Links**: Paste one or multiple product page URLs
2. **Asset Parsing**: Agent automatically visits pages and extracts all available assets and information
3. **Design Generation**: Generate multiple ad design options based on templates and product features
4. **Manual Review**: Browse results and select your preferred versions
5. **Export and Deploy**: Download desired formats and upload directly to advertising platforms

## Use Cases

- Ecommerce sellers need to quickly create large quantities of product ads
- Cross-border ecommerce teams preparing localized ads for different markets
- Small marketing teams without in-house design resources
- Ad managers who need to quickly test different asset combinations
- Dropshipping entrepreneurs looking for low-cost ways to launch ad campaigns
