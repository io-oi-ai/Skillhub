# AI Lead Generator

## Overview

AI Lead Generator is an intelligent lead prospecting skill for B2B marketing and sales teams. It leverages AI capabilities to identify potential customers from public information sources, automatically evaluate lead quality, and generate structured customer profiles, helping sales teams focus time on the most valuable business opportunities.

## Core Features

### Lead Discovery and Collection
- Discover potential customers from multiple channels based on target customer profile (ICP)
- Analyze company websites, recruitment information, news updates, and other public data to identify demand signals
- Identify key decision makers and contact information to establish effective outreach paths
- Monitor industry dynamics and company changes to capture optimal outreach timing

### Lead Scoring and Segmentation
- Automatically score leads based on multi-dimensional metrics (company size, industry match, demand signal strength, etc.)
- Segment leads into high, medium, and low priority to help sales teams allocate efforts reasonably
- Continuously track lead status changes and dynamically adjust scores
- Generate lead quality analysis reports to optimize customer acquisition strategies

### Customer Profile Generation
- Automatically consolidate public information to generate structured company and contact profiles
- Include key fields such as company basics, business scope, technology stack, and recent activities
- Analyze potential pain points companies may face, providing conversation entry point suggestions
- Recommend personalized communication strategies and talking point directions

## Typical Use Cases

1. **SaaS Customer Acquisition**: Define ideal customer profile based on product characteristics and batch prospect target companies
2. **Conference/Event Marketing**: Filter high-value prospects around conference exhibitors and attendee lists
3. **Competitor Customer Analysis**: Identify existing customers of competitors and evaluate conversion possibilities
4. **Regional Market Expansion**: Batch discover and evaluate potential customers in target regions

## Usage Examples

- "Find me 50 mid-size tech companies in Shenzhen using Kubernetes"
- "Analyze this company list and sort by purchase intent from high to low"
- "Based on this company's website and recent recruiting information, analyze their possible technology needs"
- "Generate a target customer list for the financial industry, focusing on companies undergoing digital transformation"

## Important Notes

- All lead data comes from public information sources. Please ensure usage complies with local laws and regulations.
- Lead scoring is AI-assisted judgment; final business decisions still require human confirmation.
- Recommend regularly updating target customer profiles to ensure lead prospecting aligns with business strategy.
- Works best when integrated with CRM systems to avoid lead information being scattered across multiple places.
